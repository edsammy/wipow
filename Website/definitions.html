Unit definitions for each measurement:

  • [Voltage] = RMS Volts
  • [Current] = RMS Amperes
  • [Real Power] = Watts (kilowatt for Cogen)
  • [Reactive Power] = Volt-Amps-Reactive (kiloVAR for Cogen)
  • [Apparent Power] = Volt-Amps, product of RMS Voltage and RMS Current (kiloVA for Cogen)
  • Power Factor = Ratio of Real to Apparent Power
  • [Frequency] = Hertz
  • [Phase Angle] = Degrees
  • Current THDN (Total Harmonic Distortion + Noise) = Power ratio of total phase current RMS to fundamental phase current RMS, THDN = (Irms^2 / Irms_fund^2) - 1
